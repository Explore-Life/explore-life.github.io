Venice Simplon-Orient-Express

In 1883 the Orient Express embarked on its inaugural journey, and over the years it earned renown as the world's most glamorous train. Today, the luxurious Venice Simplon-Orient-Express continues the tradition, carrying 188 guests in sophisticated style.Ornate carriages, sumptuous cuisine and attentive personal service make the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express train one of the finest luxury travel experiences in the world. 

Choose from these wonderful journeys aboard the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express!

Europe | London - Paris - Venice

London-Paris-Venice & vice versa

Embark on the British Pullman from London and travel through the Kentish countryside, before crossing the Channel. Board the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express for a journey throughout France and into Switzerland. Travels through the towering Swiss Mountains and Austrian Alps before you arrive in the magnificent city of Venice. 2 Days/1 Night

 

Istanbul - Bucharest - Budapest - Venice
Istanbul-Bucharest-Budapest-Venice
 

Embark the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express in Istanbul and travel throughout Turkey and Bulgaria. Arrive the next day in Bucharest for a night, and continue your journey towards Hungary before arriving in Budapest, the next morning. Rejoin the train and travel into Austria, stopping for a short city tour in Vienna. Enjoy your last night on board as the train crosses into Italy and on to your final destination, Venice. 6 Days/5 Nights  

 
Paris - Budapest - Bucharest - Istanbul
Paris-Budapest-Bucharest-Istanbul
 

The blue and gold carriages of the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express await you in Paris for your journey throughout France, Switzerland and Austria towards Budapest. After an overnight stay in Budapest, continue your journey through Hungary and into Romania. Enjoy the next day and night in Bucharest. On rejoining the train the next day stop in the fascinating country of Bulgaria. Enjoy your last night and day on board as the train travels to your destination, Istanbul. 6 Days/5 Nights

 

Venices - Krakow - Dresden - London

Venice - Krakow - Dresden - London

Venice-Krakow-Dresden-London

Travel across the Alps from Venice and on into the heart of Europe. Culture and adventure combine in visits to such architecturally dazzling and historically rich cities as Krakow and Dresden, set against the breathtaking landscapes of Eastern Europe. 7 Days/6 Nights
