Who we are

'explore life is the story of Christina Lando, a cane-farmer's daughter, who explored the fields as a child, innocent and free. Roaming the wilderness, Christina grew a love for discovery, and was drawn by an appreciation of the natural beauty of our wonderful world. Her imagination was as rich and wild as the berries she plucked from the mulberry tree, but no longer was it enough to satisfy her longing heart and inquisitive mind.'

Founder & Director, Christina Lando re-branded the original travel consultancy business that she established in 2002, to the now fresh-faced Explore Life - Travel Design.



explore life is based in Brisbane, Australia.  Many of our clients are located throughout the world and across Australia.  With over 20 years experience taking care and designing travel arrangements for leisure and corporate travellers, we find our clients value the personalised attention and knowledgeable advice we give, so they give us the opportunity to help their friends.   We take good care of our clients, so for their own reasons they take good care of us - referring many new travellers to us!

We are travel specialists in designing individual experiences for you, wherever it may be in our world. 

We still love to travel and we find pleasure in taking care of your travel arrangements.  There is nothing more satisfying in our profession than our client's returning from their trips excited and wanting to share their stories.  Their gratitude and feedback is invaluable.

We have the experience in business.  We've dealt directly in problem solving our client's travels during disastrous world events, such as, the Gulf & Iraq wars, SARS, Airline collapses, 9/11, Interlaken canyoning tragedy, Tsunami, the list goes on.  Responding immediately to our clients needs, providing factual information and insight and support to their families, has resulted and moulded us into how we do business today.

It is highly important to us that your travel whether for business or for a holiday, gives you the best experience possible!

Ultimately, we are proud.  We care.  We deliver.
