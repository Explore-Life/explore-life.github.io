The Globus family of brands provides a variety of vacations addressing the needs of today's diverse traveler with unparalleled vacation experiences, full of discovery and insight.  Avalon Waterways (their sister brand) welcomes travellers to try the best river cruises in the industry.

To allow everyone to experience their amazing river vacations, Avalon Waterways are offering a couples fly to Europe when they book one of the following three cruises: 28 March & 13 June 2010 (Budapest to Amsterdam) and 25 July 2010 (Amsterdam to Budapest).

Please note, this sale is only available until 31 March 2010 and is subject to availability. Savings are per person twin share. The free airfare including taxes is based on a special with Lufthansa Airlines. For more information about the routing or this special please contact us.
