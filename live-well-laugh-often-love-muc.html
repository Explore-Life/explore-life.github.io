Live well, laugh often, love much!

At Explore Life we know our people!  We know you want excitement, entertainment, great times, great laughs, and great value for money!  You've asked for it, and you're going to get it!  We're pleased to announce the birth of a concept.... Explore Life Mystery Tours!  

The plan is this....

 

 * to get groups of people together (friends, clients, friends of friends....soon to all be the best of friends!).  Let's call it a gathering, a coming together of groups of quality people for quality times!

* to arrange periodic Mystery Tours - we can't tell you anything here or we may have to kill you (!!), but put it this way,

                some might be a day on a weekend, some might be a Friday evening

                some might involve an activity/meal/refreshments

                some might involve some travel/transport

All you get to know before hand, is the date, details for meeting/departing points, what suitable attire to don, and the cost involved for the mysterious arrangements we make!

Mystery Tours will be suitable if you are maried, partnered or single.  

Stay tuned!  Numbers will always be limited so it will be a case of first in best dressed!  Don't miss out on securing your spots quickly!  It could be the best thing you've ever done!
